# Retro Glow Pong Brand Kit

## Brand Mood

Retro Glow Pong should feel like a premium 1998 internet arcade cabinet running on a modern OLED phone.

## Colors

- OLED Black: `#000000`
- Plasma Cyan: `#4AFFE7`
- Arcade Magenta: `#FF4CCD`
- Glass Violet: `#A574FF`
- Score White: `#FFFFFF`
- Soft Chrome: `#DDE9FF`

## Typography

Use bold geometric sans-serif text. The prototype uses system fonts to stay dependency-free.

## Visual Rules

- Background stays black.
- Gameplay elements glow, but the screen should not become muddy or overfilled.
- Cyan belongs to the player.
- Magenta belongs to the CPU.
- White belongs to score, ball highlights, and collision flash.
