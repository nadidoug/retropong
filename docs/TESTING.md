# Testing Checklist

## Browser Smoke Test

1. Open `index.html`.
2. Confirm the canvas fills the screen.
3. Move the mouse or finger vertically.
4. Confirm the left paddle follows the input.
5. Confirm the CPU paddle follows the ball.
6. Confirm the ball bounces off paddles and top/bottom walls.
7. Let one side miss.
8. Confirm the score changes and the ball resets.
9. Keep a rally going.
10. Confirm the ball gets faster.

## Mobile Test

1. Open the game on a phone browser.
2. Hold the phone in portrait.
3. Confirm the rotate-to-landscape prompt appears.
4. Rotate the phone to landscape.
5. Confirm the game fills the landscape browser viewport.
6. Drag vertically anywhere on the canvas.
7. Confirm the paddle tracks smoothly.
8. Confirm the page does not scroll while playing.

## Expected Console Result

No JavaScript errors.
